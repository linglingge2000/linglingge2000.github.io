一、范德蒙德矩阵(Vandermonde matrix)

[TOC]

1. 定义

对于标量$x_1,x_2,\dots,x_n\in \mathbb{C}$，范德蒙德矩阵$V_n=\begin{bmatrix}1&1&\cdots&1\x_1& x_2&\cdots&x_n\ x_1^2&x_2^2&\cdots&x_n^2\\vdots&\vdots&\ddots&\vdots\x_1^{n-1}&x_2^{n-1}&\cdots&x_n^{n-1}\end{bmatrix}$

2. 范德蒙德行列式推导证明

定理：若V为n维范德蒙德方阵，则其行列式$det(V_n)=\Pi_{1\leq j < i \leq n}(x_i-x_j)$

证明：用数学归纳法证明如下。

当n=2时，$det(V_2)=\begin{vmatrix}1&1\x_1&x_2\end{vmatrix}=x_2-x_1$;

当n=3时，

$\det(V_3)=\begin{vmatrix}1&1&1\x_1&x_2&x_3\x_1^2&x_2^2&x_3^2\end{vmatrix}\=(-1)^{1+1}\begin{vmatrix}1&1&1\0&x_2-x_1&x_3-x_1\0&x_2(x_2-x_1)&x_3(x_3-x_1)\end{vmatrix}\=(x_2-x_1)\begin{vmatrix}1&1\x_2&x_3\end{vmatrix}=(x_2-x_1)(x_3-x_2);$

假设当n=n-1时，该结论成立；

当n=n时，

$\det(V_n)=\begin{vmatrix}1&1&\cdots&1\0& x_2-x_1&\cdots&x_n-x_1\ 0&x_2^2-x_1^2&\cdots&x_n^2-x_1^2\\vdots&\vdots&\ddots&\vdots\0&x_2^{n-1}-x_1^{n-1}&\cdots&x_n^{n-1}-x_1^{n-1}\end{vmatrix}\=(-1)^{1+1}\begin{vmatrix}1&1&\cdots&1\0& x_2-x_1&\cdots&x_n-x_1\ 0&x_2(x_2-x_1)&\cdots&x_n(x_n-x_1)\\vdots&\vdots&\ddots&\vdots\0&x_2^{n-2}(x_2-x_1)&\cdots&x_n^{n-2}(x_n-x_1)\end{vmatrix}\=(x_2-x_1)(x_3-x_1)\cdots(x_n-x_1)\begin{vmatrix}1&1&\cdots&1\x_1& x_2&\cdots&x_n\ x_1^2&x_2^2&\cdots&x_n^2\\vdots&\vdots&\ddots&\vdots\x_1^{n-2}&x_2^{n-2}&\cdots&x_n^{n-2}\end{vmatrix}\=(x_2-x_1)(x_3-x_1)\cdots(x_n-x_1)det(V_{n-1});$

得证！
